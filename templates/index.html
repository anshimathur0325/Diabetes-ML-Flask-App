What is diabetes?

  • Diabetes is a disease that occurs when the body cannot produce insulin or can't regulate its production.
  • It is long-lasting and can cause serious problems like heart disease and vision loss.

The earlier someone identifies their risk of diabetes, the sooner they can start receiving necessary treatment.
